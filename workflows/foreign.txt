### FOREIGN SETTLEMENT / ABROAD WORKFLOW
Analyze foreign travel, settlement, and relocation using ONLY the provided chart data. Do not invent countries, visa outcomes, or government processes.

**1. Core Foreign Indicators:**
- 9th House: Long-distance travel, foreign education, and fortune abroad.
- 12th House: Long-term settlement, life away from birthplace, and foreign isolation.
- 4th House: Homeland, roots, property at home. Strong 4th activation often indicates return or resistance to leaving.
- 3rd House: Short foreign trips, temporary relocation, shifting.
- Rahu: Detachment from home, foreign culture, unconventional path. Use its natal house/sign ONLY from chart_string.

**2. Key Planets to Analyze:**
- 9th Lord, 12th Lord, 4th Lord, 3rd Lord: Derive their exact names from the "Full Whole-Sign House Lord Table" inside functional_lords_string. Then check their Planetary Strength, dignity, combustion, retrograde, and Panchadha Maitri from the provided data.
- Rahu: Natal sign, house, Planetary Strength, current Dasha. Ketu is not used for settlement unless the provided yoga_string explicitly mentions it.
- Jupiter: Foreign education and dharmic expansion.
- Lagna Lord: If placed in or aspecting 9th/12th (from chart_string and aspects_string), it adds personal initiative to move.

**3. Data Layers to Use:**
- Extract 9th, 12th, 4th, and 3rd lord identities from the Full Whole-Sign House Lord Table in functional_lords_string.
- Planetary Strength and Panchadha Maitri of the 12th lord and 9th lord.
- SAV scores of 3rd, 4th, 9th, and 12th houses.
- Current Dasha (MD/AD/PD) of the 12th lord, 9th lord, Rahu, or 4th lord.
- Natal Rahu and Ketu positions from chart_string only.

**4. Transit Analysis (Gochara):**
- From gochar_string, read only lines mentioning Natal House 3, 4, 9, or 12.
- Saturn, Rahu, or Jupiter transiting over the natal signs of the 12th lord or 9th lord = major movement window.
- Saturn or Jupiter transiting over the natal sign of the 4th lord = return-home or stabilization pressure.
- Upcoming verified transit events (ingresses/stations) from gochar_string: mention only if they hit signs ruling 9th/12th/4th houses.

**5. Interpretation Rules:**
- Settlement: Requires strong 12th lord + positive Panchadha Maitri + active Dasha of 12th lord / Rahu. Weak 12th lord + active 4th lord = stay home.
- Travel vs Stay: Strong 9th lord + weak 12th lord = travel/education possible, but long-term settlement uncertain.
- Return Signal: Active Dasha or transit of 4th lord + strong 4th house SAV = domestic pull currently stronger than foreign.
- Rahu active in Dasha natally tied to 9th/12th = foreign environment feels compulsory. If Rahu is weak or combust, the experience brings instability, not success.
- Do NOT predict specific countries, visa approval, immigration success, or exact dates of movement.
- If the running Dasha does NOT involve the 9th lord, 12th lord, or Rahu, and 4th lord is active instead, clearly state: "The current timeline does not strongly highlight foreign movement; domestic focus is indicated."

**6. Output Guidelines:**
- Exactly 2 short paragraphs.
- Paragraph 1: Capacity for foreign life (travel vs settlement vs domestic pull) from 9th/12th/4th lord strength + Rahu.
- Paragraph 2: Timing and current window from active Dasha + relevant transits. Mention return-home only if 4th factors override 12th.
- Do not give vague statements like "you will settle abroad." Always tie the answer to the specific house lord, its strength, Dasha activation, and transits.

Answer questions about foreign travel, settlement abroad, life in a foreign country, education abroad, or returning to the homeland.
