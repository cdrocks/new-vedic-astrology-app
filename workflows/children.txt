### CHILDREN & FAMILY WORKFLOW
Analyze children, conception, pregnancy, and family expansion using ONLY the provided chart data. This is an astrological outlook only, not medical or fertility advice.

**1. Core Children Indicators:**
- 5th House: Progeny, conception potential, and relationship with children.
- 5th Lord: Read this from the "Full Whole-Sign House Lord Table" in the Functional Lords section. Do not guess.
- Jupiter: Natural Putra-karaka. Its strength and current Dasha status are mandatory for every progeny reading.
- Putrakaraka (PK): From the provided Chara Karaka list. Reveals karmic patterns with children.
- Saturn / Rahu: If natally in the 5th house or aspecting the 5th lord (use chart_string and aspects_string only), indicate delay, structure, or unconventional paths. Do not invent their presence.
- Moon: Emotional bonding and nurturing capacity.

**2. Key Planets & Data to Check:**
- 5th Lord: dignity, combustion, retrograde, Panchadha Maitri, Planetary Strength.
- Jupiter: Planetary Strength, D9 sign (from d9_string), active Dasha.
- Putrakaraka (PK): house, sign, dignity, and D9 sign (cross-reference karaka_string with d9_string).
- Any planet explicitly listed in House 5: read from chart_string only.

**3. Data Layers to Use:**
- Extract 5th Lord name from the Full Whole-Sign House Lord Table. Then check its Planetary Strength and Panchadha Maitri.
- SAV score of the 5th house (from ashtakavarga_string). < 20 = low support.
- Current Dasha (MD/AD/PD) of 5th lord, Jupiter, or PK. If none are active, say so directly.
- D9 dignity of 5th lord and PK. Better D9 than D1 = hidden resilience. Worse D9 = added friction.
- Do NOT invoke Saptamsa (D7), Beeja Sphuta, Kshetra Sphuta, or any unseen divisional chart.

**4. Transit Analysis (Gochara):**
- From gochar_string, use only lines mentioning "Natal House 5".
- Jupiter transiting the 5th lord's natal sign or natal House 5: support window IF natal Jupiter / 5th lord are not severely weak.
- Saturn or Rahu transiting over House 5 or the 5th lord sign: delay or pressure. Mention only if gochar_string shows it.
- Use ONLY the upcoming verified transit events listed; do not invent dates.

**5. Interpretation Rules:**
- Strong 5th lord + strong Jupiter + well-placed PK + active Dasha of these = favorable progeny momentum.
- Weak 5th lord BUT strong Jupiter + active Jupiter Dasha = possible through patience or support systems.
- Natal Saturn/Rahu in/aspecting 5th (data-proven only) + weak 5th lord + no Jupiter relief = structured delay. Never say "impossible"; say "requires aligned timing."
- Active Dasha does NOT touch 5th lord, Jupiter, or PK = children matters are not currently highlighted.
- Do NOT state exact number of children. The data does not reliably support enumeration.

**6. Output Guidelines:**
- Exactly 2 short paragraphs.
- Paragraph 1: Current progeny potential from 5th lord + Jupiter + PK strength and dignity.
- Paragraph 2: Timing and active window from Dasha + relevant transits. If no activation of 5th lord, Jupiter, or PK in the current Dasha, state clearly: "The running timeline does not currently highlight child-related momentum."
- Do not give fertility advice, medical recommendations, or guarantee conception.
