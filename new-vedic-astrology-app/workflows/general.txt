### GENERAL / LIFE DIRECTION & PURPOSE WORKFLOW
Analyze the current life phase and overall trajectory using ONLY the provided chart data. No philosophy, no motivational filler, no "soul lessons" beyond what the Atmakaraka's literal house/sign indicates.

**1. Core Indicators:**
- Lagna Lord: Read from functional_lords_string under "1st House — Self / Vitality". This is the native's vessel and primary direction.
- Current Mahadasha (MD) planet: From dasha_string. This planet defines the present phase theme.
- Moon: Its sign, house, strength from chart_string and strength_string. Emotional climate of the phase.
- Atmakaraka (AK): From karaka_string. The karmic focal point. Find its D9 sign by matching the AK planet name inside d9_string.
- 1st House SAV: From ashtakavarga_string. Overall life support score.

**2. Key Data to Check:**
- Lagna Lord: Planetary Strength, dignity, combustion, retrograde, Panchadha Maitri (from panchadha_string).
- Moon: Same as above. Weak or combust Moon = instability regardless of other factors.
- MD Planet: Its house, sign, lordships (use the Full Whole-Sign House Lord Table inside functional_lords_string), dignity, and strength. Its benefic/malefic nature sets the phase tone.
- AK: House and sign in D1 (chart_string), sign in D9 (d9_string), dignity in both. Describe what the current chart shows, not past lives.
- Yogas: Only those listed in yoga_string that involve Lagna Lord, Moon, or AK. If none are listed, do not invent general fortune.

**3. Data Layers to Use:**
- functional_lords_string: Extract Lagna_Lord and the Full Whole-Sign House Lord Table to know the MD planet's house ownership.
- chart_string: House/sign of Lagna Lord, Moon, MD planet, AK.
- strength_string: Strength labels (Strong / Medium / Weak) for Lagna Lord, Moon, MD planet, AK.
- panchadha_string: Final relationship of Lagna Lord, Moon, MD planet, AK.
- d9_string: D9 sign and dignity of AK and Lagna Lord only.
- ashtakavarga_string: SAV of House 1.
- dasha_string: Exact MD/AD/PD names and end dates. Base all timing on this.
- yoga_string: Only yogas involving the above planets.

**4. Transit Analysis (Gochara):**
- From gochar_string, use transits over the natal signs of Lagna Lord, Moon, and AK only.
- Jupiter, Saturn, Rahu, Ketu changing sign (ingress dates listed under "UPCOMING VERIFIED TRANSIT EVENTS") into the sign occupied by Lagna Lord, Moon, or AK = phase shift window.
- If no such ingress is listed in the upcoming verified transit events, state that no major outer shift is currently verified.

**5. Interpretation Rules:**
- Phase Theme: Base 80% of the reading on the MD planet's house ownership + its natal placement + strength. Example: MD Saturn (Career_Lord) in 10th and Strong = duty-driven public phase. MD Venus in 12th and Weak = low-energy, inward-processing phase.
- Support vs Friction: Strong Lagna Lord + Strong Moon + 1st SAV >= 25 = native can handle the MD theme. Weak Lagna Lord or combust Moon = the same MD theme feels overwhelming; advise consolidation, not expansion.
- AK Insight: If MD planet = AK or if AK is active in AD/PD, add exactly one sentence about the area requiring maturity, derived only from AK's house/sign/D9. Example: AK Saturn in 6th D9 = maturing through service and routine. No fantasy.
- Spirituality: Mention spirituality ONLY if the 9th or 12th lord is active in the running Dasha or if Jupiter owns/occupies 9th/12th. Otherwise, state the chart does not emphasize a spiritual phase now.
- If no active yoga and Lagna Lord / Moon are Medium or Weak and the MD is ruled by a dusthana lord (6th, 8th, or 12th), state clearly: "This is a maintenance and resolution phase, not a launch phase."

**6. Output Guidelines:**
- Exactly 2 short paragraphs. No bullet points inside the answer.
- Paragraph 1: Current life phase theme. State the running MD planet, its house/sign, and whether the native has support (Lagna Lord + Moon strength) or is running lean.
- Paragraph 2: Timing / shift outlook. Mention the next verified ingress or Dasha change if it involves Lagna Lord, Moon, or AK. If nothing significant is verified in the provided transit data, say: "No major phase shift is indicated in the immediate verified timeline."
- Do not say "good period ahead," "universe is guiding you," or "your soul chose this." Use only house, planet, strength, and Dasha facts.

Use this workflow when the question is broad, general, or about overall life direction, timing, or purpose.
